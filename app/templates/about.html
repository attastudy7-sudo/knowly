About EduShare

Empowering students through knowledge sharing

Our Mission

EduShare is a modern educational social media platform designed to help students share knowledge, collaborate on learning materials, and build a supportive academic community. We believe that education is better when it's shared.

Features

Share Documents

Upload and share educational materials including PDFs, Word documents, and presentations.

Connect with Peers

Follow other students, engage with their content, and build your learning network.

Engage & Discuss

Like, comment, and discuss educational content with the community.

Mobile-First

Optimized for mobile devices with a clean, responsive design.

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. All passwords are securely hashed, and your personal information is protected. We use industry-standard security practices to keep your data safe.
